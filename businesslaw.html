    Business Law

    Knudsen & Simpson advises individuals, partnerships, corporations, and other types of business entities on a varied range of business-related legal issues. These issues range from business formation, to business transactions, administration, and dissolution.

    Business Formation

    Whether you are thinking about starting a business, or thinking about transitioning to a different type of business entity, Knudsen & Simpson will assist you throughout the entire process. We will counsel you on which type of entity will best fit your needs, including your business objectives, management style, and risk tolerance.

    Business Representation

    Every business is unique, and has different needs. We strive to understand the needs of every business and tailor our services based upon the particulars of every client. Whether your business requires a small one-time transaction, or continuing legal guidance, we will be with you and your business every step of the way.

    One of our new services is our Business Counsel Plan. By enrolling in the Business Counsel Plan for a small yearly fee, your business will have constant access to legal advice, as if we were your very own in-house counsel.

    Business Litigation

    In reality, most businesses at one time or another may find itself in need of litigation counsel. We at Knudsen & Simpson will always be in your corner to represent you in every aspect of the litigation process. We will zealously represent you and your business in court to ensure the best possible legal outcome.
